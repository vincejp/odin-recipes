Fettucine Alfredo

Description

A homecooked fettuccine alfredo recipe, courtesy of Food Network's Ree Drummond.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound fettuccine noodles
  • 1 stick butter
  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • Salt and freshly ground black pepper
  • 2 cups freshly grated Parmesan

Steps

  1. Cook the pasta according to package directions.
  2. In a saucepan or skillet over low heat, warm the butter and cream. Season with salt and pepper.
  3. Place half of the Parmesan into a large serving bowl.
  4. Pour the warm butter/cream mixture over the top.
  5. Drain the pasta and immediately pour it into the bowl.
  6. Toss a couple of times, and then sprinkle in the other half of the Parmesan.
  7. Toss to combine, thinning with pasta water if necessary.
  8. Serve immediately as a main course or accompaniment to meat or salad. Delicious!
